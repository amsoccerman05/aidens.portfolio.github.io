FRC 4418 Impulse

First Robotics Competition team 4418 Impulse is a robotics team based out of STEM School Highlands Ranch. Every year we design, fabricate, code, and compete with a 150lb robot. I am the Program Manager, executing the teams goals through the engineering design process. Part of being the Program Manger is leading our team of 30 people using Agile Methodologies such as Gantt Charts and KanBan boards. I led our team to the most succesful year to date, being semi-finalists twice and winning our first award!
